Intro

It is okay to Stay Silent. Conversations should flow naturally. When Students start off fresh in universities, they will have to meet a lot of new people and make new friends.

But there will always be a time where you want to talk with a specific person but find it to be difficult to find a topic to talk about.

When you need help Social+ is here for you with the tips and ideas and can support and also give new suggestions to users on topics they could use to get closer to others.
